# Company Context — Ngern Turbo (เงินเทอร์โบ)

## Overview
- **Company:** Ngern Turbo Co., Ltd.
- **Group:** Turbo Group (เทอร์โบ กรุ๊ป)
- **Industry:** Financial Services — Consumer Finance & Leasing
- **Country:** Thailand

## Core Business
Consumer financial products:
- Auto loans and vehicle financing (cars, motorcycles)
- Hire-purchase agreements
- Equipment leasing
- Personal loans
- Bundled insurance products

**Customers:** Middle-income individuals in Thailand seeking vehicle or equipment financing, through direct and dealer channels.

## Technology Landscape
- **Current ERP:** SAP — being phased out at the PO module level
- **Internal Services:** Bookkeeping service (internal accounting platform)
- **Direction:** Build internal systems to replace SAP modules and reduce licensing dependency

## Regulatory Context (Thailand)
- Subject to BOT (Bank of Thailand) oversight for lending activities
- VAT 7% applies to vendor purchases
- Withholding Tax (WHT / ภาษีหัก ณ ที่จ่าย) must be calculated and certified at payment
- e-Tax invoice integration with Revenue Department — future requirement

## Organizational Notes
- Approval authority is role-based and amount-threshold-based
- CFO approval is required for high-value purchases (threshold configurable)
- Finance team (Accountants, Financial Controller) handles coding and PO approval
- Procurement team manages vendor relationships and quotations
